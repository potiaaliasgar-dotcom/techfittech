TechFit — Gym Setup, Equipment Sourcing, Fabrication & Wellness Solutions India

TechFit (Techfit Health Fitness Private Limited) is India's leading comprehensive gym setup partner, custom sports equipment manufacturer, and advanced wellness recovery infrastructure supplier. Headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, TechFit offers turnkey gym setup solutions, fitness equipment supply, and custom fabrication services across all major cities in India, including Mumbai, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Surat, Goa, Jaipur, Lucknow, and Chandigarh.

As the official authorised distributor of premium global brands like BH Fitness, Tunturi, California Fitness, and Alteon Wellness (alteontech.com), TechFit brings world-class quality to Indian fitness amenities. We supply premium European cardio, cloud-connected strength machines, Nordic fitness gear, and light-commercial conditioning packages to commercial health clubs, five-star hotel gym amenities, residential developers, corporate gym sites, and school/institutional fitness facilities across India.

TechFit custom-manufactures competition-grade combat sports equipment, functional training structures, and sports amenities at our local facility in Mumbai. TechFit is the official MMA cage and boxing ring builder for India's leading fight promotions, including Matrix Fight Night (MFN), Super Fight League (SFL), and Kumite 1 League, and is the choice provider for premium athletic training gyms like Tiger Shroff's signature MMA Matrix. We manufacture heavy-duty CrossFit functional training rigs using laser-cut 11-gauge steel, free weights, Olympic barbells with precision knurling and needle bearings, dumbbells, shock-absorbing gym flooring, SS316 marine-grade aqua fitness pool equipment, and custom racket sports infrastructure including ITF-compliant padel and pickleball courts.

Our complete longevity and wellness recovery portfolio features Alteon hyperbaric oxygen chambers (HBOT), electric whole-body cryotherapy chambers, clinical photobiomodulation (PBM) red light therapy panels, zero-gravity dry floatation beds, far-infrared wellness saunas, and cellular interval hypoxic-hyperoxic training (IHHT) active altitude systems. With over 300 successful commercial gyms, real estate developer amenities, hotel gym setups, and school fitness installations delivered since 2016, TechFit provides end-to-end gym design consulting, equipment supply, professional installation, and comprehensive AMC maintenance support.
